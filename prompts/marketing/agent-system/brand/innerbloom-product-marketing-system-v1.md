# Innerbloom Product Marketing System v1

Commercial objective: generate leads, new users, app downloads, and account creation.

Primary audience: people who want to build habits but repeatedly stop halfway when energy, time, or context changes.

Core promise: **See your progress, adjust your challenge, and build habits sustainably.**

Brand voice: an intelligent friend who does not judge.

Primary emotional outcome: hope. Desired reaction: “Finally, someone understands why I stop.”

Message order:
1. Recognition of the real problem.
2. Reframe consistency without perfection.
3. Explain the product mechanism.
4. Prove it with truthful UI.
5. Ask for download and account creation.

Default campaign and visible asset language: English unless strategy explicitly approves another language.

A post fails when it is generic, the screenshot does not prove the claim, it explains a false capability, creates pressure, has no funnel function, or has no clear next action.
