# Innerbloom Visual System v2

## Source hierarchy
1. Current approved landing and application screenshots.
2. Approved logo and exported assets in Drive.
3. Current CSS and design tokens.
4. This document.
5. Historical material as loose reference only.

## Brand character
Calm, premium, adaptive, clean, product-led, emotionally aware, and visually distinctive.

Avoid generic wellness pastels, Canva-template layouts, flat SaaS styling, aggressive neon, green-beige lifestyle branding, and cold enterprise dashboards.

## Typography
- Headings: Sora, weight 700–800, tight but readable.
- Body/support: Manrope, weight 400–500.
- Wordmark: always use the approved logo asset. Never recreate or type it with a substitute font.
- Social typography must match the landing family and remain readable at mobile preview size.

## Colour
Dark mode:
- base #06060B
- surface #0D0C14
- primary text #F3F0FA
- violet #8E63FF
- lilac #C88DFF
- peach #F2AE9B

Light mode:
- base #F1E9DE
- surface #FBF8F3
- primary text #211C2B
- violet #8E63FF
- lilac #C88DFF
- peach #F2AE9B

Functional colours preserve product meaning. Green is functional, not the primary brand colour. Do not use pure white as the default light background.

## Composition
- One dominant focal point.
- Product UI is the primary proof element.
- Use generous but intentional negative space.
- Maintain strong hierarchy and mobile readability.
- Rounded cards, restrained borders, subtle glow, and atmospheric depth are allowed.
- Avoid decorative clutter and empty unfinished layouts.

## Screenshot treatment
Allowed: crop, zoom, reframe, resize, restrained device frame, dimming outside the focal area, subtle glow, truthful callouts, and compositions using several real screenshots.

Forbidden: changing visible values, fabricating charts, inventing controls, rewriting labels, hallucinating UI, or using a screenshot as decoration unrelated to the claim.

## Module art direction
- Daily Energy: make the chart legible; preserve HP, Focus, Mood and their functional colours; communicate accumulated momentum.
- Emotion Chart: preserve emotion colours and use it only for emotional patterns and perspective over time.
- Dashboard/tasks/weekly charts: use for progress, rhythm performance, and balance.
- Rhythm selection: use for onboarding and realistic target explanation, not weekly reset.
- Habit Achieved: use for earned, shareable progress.

## Static posts
Exactly one final image. Preferred patterns: editorial headline plus product proof, full-screen product crop with minimal framing, or one module spotlight with one truthful callout.

## Carousels
Produce every approved slide. Use varied layouts within one coherent family. Recommended sequence: problem, reframe, mechanism, product proof, CTA. Use at least two real product states when relevant.

## Final quality gate
The asset must feel unmistakably Innerbloom, use approved typography and logo, match dark/light sources correctly, prove the claim with real UI, remain legible on mobile, and look ready for professional publication.
