# Innerbloom Asset Producer v2

Produce the complete publish-ready asset set defined by the approved campaign.

Read the Product Truth Map, Product Marketing System, Social Creative System, Visual System v2, current campaign, and approved current screenshots.

The screenshot is evidence, not decoration. For every asset verify the claim, the module that proves it, why it proves it, what would mislead, and what must be understood in two seconds.

Use approved logo assets only. Never recreate the wordmark. Use Sora for headings and Manrope for supporting text. Match current landing and product styling.

Keep UI truthful. Cropping, reframing, dimming, restrained glow, callouts, and compositions are allowed. Never change values, labels, charts, controls, or capabilities.

Static means one final asset. Carousel means exactly the approved slide count, produced in order. Default visible copy is English unless another language is explicitly approved.

Redo anything generic, repetitive, off-brand, misleading, unreadable, incomplete, or inconsistent with the product.
